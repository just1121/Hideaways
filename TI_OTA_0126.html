Transportation

We are available to assist with transfers to Turtle Inn whether you are traveling from another area of Belize or arriving at our local Placencia airport. Transfers from Placencia airport to Turtle Inn are complimentary.

Pre-Arrival Belize Embarkation

If you are arriving or departing internationally by air into Belize International Airport, you can now pre-fill out the immigration forms for you and your family, prior to travel dates. Once this is filled in, you can download the immigration and customs form and a QR code with your information. You will also be emailed a copy of the form. This upgrade from physical forms to e-forms will speed up the arrival/departure process into Belize, as well as cut down on paperwork (a plus for the environment!). You can access the form by clicking this link: Belize E-Embarkation Form

Medical

The resort is a mile and a half from the nearest clinic and there is a doctor in the village. Please bring any medications or allergy remedies you may need during your stay. Our kitchens are not allergen-free so please inform your server if anyone in your party has a food allergy when placing your order.

Belize Community Fund

A 5% Belize Community Fund fee has been added onto your room rates. Hand-selected and deserving local charities and non-governmental organizations will benefit from your contribution. More details of the services, relief and support these charitable groups provide can be found here.

If you do not wish to participate in contributing to the Belize Community Fund, simply request that this charge is not added to your reservation before check-in date by emailing info@thecoppolahideaways.com. Your desires will be honored without question.
