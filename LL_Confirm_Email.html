Transportation

A 1 hour flight takes you from Guatemala City to Flores International Airport. La Lancha is a 45 minute drive from the airport. We have two local airlines (AVIANCA and TAG) that have scheduled flights from Flores to Guatemala City. You may also take a boat from Flores to La Lancha (approximately 35 minutes). We can arrange to have a driver meet you at the airport and transfer you at an additional charge, or you can rent a car at either Tabarini or Hertz, which are two auto rentals just in front of the Flores Airport.

Pre-arrival Electronic Regional Traveler Affidavit

If you are arriving internationally by air into Guatemala City Airport, you can now pre-fill out the immigration forms for you and your family, up to 3 days before travel dates. Once this is filled in, you can download the declaration form number and QR code with your information. You will also be emailed a copy of the form. This upgrade from physical forms to e-forms will speed up the arrival process into Guatemala, as well as cut down on paperwork (a plus for the environment!)
